Artist Onboarding — Worldwide

From application to first placement.

Open to artists anywhere in the world. A selective roster, personally reviewed — here's exactly how joining works.

1

Apply

Tell us about your practice with the form below — discipline, portfolio, and what drives the work. Five minutes, no fee.

2

Curatorial review

Every application is reviewed personally by our founder — for originality, depth, and a distinct point of view. We respond within a few weeks.

3

Onboarding & representation

A conversation about your goals, then a clear representation agreement — what we do for you, and what stays yours. Your IP remains your own.

4

Commissions, exhibitions, collectors

We place your work where it belongs — murals and site commissions, brand collaborations, curated shows, and direct collector introductions.

Submitting opens your email client with your details pre-filled, addressed to our curatorial team — send it to complete your registration. We personally review every application and respond within a few weeks.
