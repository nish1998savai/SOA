Common Questions

Before you collect.

How do I know a piece is authentic?

Every piece commissioned or sourced through Society of Artists is delivered with a signed Certificate of Authenticity naming the artist, medium, and date, alongside an independent condition report completed before handover.

What happens if a work is damaged in transit?

All shipments are professionally packed and insured, with a documented condition report completed before dispatch and a shipping indemnity process in place should any damage occur in transit.

Can I meet the artist behind a piece?

Yes. Members of our collector circle get direct, ongoing introductions to roster artists, including private studio visits where the artist and schedule allow.

How are artists selected for the roster?

SOA operates a curated roster, not an open marketplace. Every artist is personally reviewed for originality, depth, and a distinct creative point of view before being represented.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes — shipping and logistics are arranged per piece and destination, with costs and timelines confirmed before dispatch.

Can I commission a custom piece?

Yes. We manage custom commissions end to end — creative direction, artist assignment, production, and installation for murals, sculpture, and site-specific work.
