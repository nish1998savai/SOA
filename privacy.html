Privacy Policy.

Last updated: 20 July 2026

Society of Artists ("SOA", "we", "us"), based in Mumbai, India, operates societyofartists.in. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. By using this website you agree to this policy.

1. Information we collect

  • Information you give us. When you submit our artist registration, contact, or mailing-list forms, the details you enter (such as your name, email address, phone number, location, portfolio links, and message) are sent to us by email. We receive only what you choose to send.
  • Technical information. This is a static website. We do not set our own cookies and we do not run analytics or advertising trackers. Our hosting provider (GitHub Pages) may collect standard server logs, including IP addresses, for security and operational purposes, under its own privacy terms.

2. How we use your information

  • To review artist applications and respond to enquiries.
  • To send community updates you have asked for (previews, spotlights, event invitations). You can opt out at any time by replying to any email.
  • To operate, protect, and improve our services.

3. What we don't do

  • We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information.
  • We do not share your details with third parties except where needed to run our services (for example, email providers) or where the law requires it.

4. Retention

We keep correspondence and application details only as long as needed for the purposes above, or as required by law. You may ask us to delete your information at any time.

5. Your rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of the personal information we hold about you by writing to nishisavai@societyofartists.in. We will respond within a reasonable time and in accordance with applicable Indian law, including the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 where it applies.

6. Third-party links

Our site links to third-party platforms (such as Instagram and WhatsApp). Their privacy practices are their own; we encourage you to review their policies.

7. Children

This website is not directed at children under 18, and we do not knowingly collect their personal information. Workshop registrations for minors are handled by their parents or guardians.

8. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The "last updated" date above reflects the current version, and continued use of the site constitutes acceptance of any changes.
