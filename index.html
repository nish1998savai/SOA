About SOA

Built for artists who deserved more than the gallery wall.

Society of Artists began with a simple observation: important artists were going unseen — not for lack of talent, but for lack of visibility, access, and the right rooms to be seen in. We work across painting, sculpture, installation, and textile art — for established artists, emerging voices, and everyone drawn to creativity.

Read our story
"I started Society of Artists because I felt there were important artists whose work deserved to be seen more deeply, spoken about more often, and placed in rooms where it truly belonged." — Nishi Savai, Founder & Curator
