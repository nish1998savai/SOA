Our Story

Built for artists who deserved more than the gallery wall.

Society of Artists is a Mumbai-based curation and commissioning collective — working across painting, sculpture, installation, and textile art.

Why We Exist

Visibility, access, and the right room.

Society of Artists began with a simple observation: important artists were going unseen — not for lack of talent, but for lack of visibility, access, and the right rooms to be seen in. We built a considered space where art moves beyond the canvas into conversation, connection, and culture — for established artists, emerging voices, and everyone drawn to creativity.

Our approach is selective, cultural, and thoughtful. We prioritise long-term creative growth over short-term exposure — placing artists in brand collaborations, exhibitions, hospitality and wellness spaces, and collector introductions built to last.

"I started Society of Artists because I felt there were important artists whose work deserved to be seen more deeply, spoken about more often, and placed in rooms where it truly belonged." — Nishi Savai, Founder & Curator
What We Do

Creative direction, curated and executed end to end.

From concept to installation, we manage every layer of a commission — creative direction, artist assignment, production, and on-site execution — so the work arrives exactly as it was imagined.

Murals & Site Commissions

Concept development, artist curation, production coordination, and full on-site execution for murals and large-format work.

Brand Collaborations

Art woven into fashion, hospitality, wellness, and lifestyle spaces — campaigns and installations built around a brand's world.

Exhibitions & Showcases

Curated shows and cultural programming that give artists room, context, and an audience that stays.

Creative Experiences & Workshops

Artist-led workshops and private events for clubs, hotels, and wellness spaces — designed around the room, not a template.

Art Sourcing & Acquisition

Original works and prints from our roster, matched to a space's purpose and character — framing handled end to end.

Collector Introductions

Thoughtful introductions between artists and collectors, built for long-term relationships rather than one-off sales.
